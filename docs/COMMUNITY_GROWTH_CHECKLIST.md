# Community Growth Checklist

## Weekly Loop

1. Ship one meaningful improvement release.
2. Publish one technical update post with a real before/after outcome.
3. Triage all new issues in under 24 hours.
4. Mark at least one issue as `good first issue`.
5. Thank and spotlight contributors publicly.

## Metrics To Track

- Views and unique visitors
- Stars / forks per week
- Clone count and unique cloners
- Time-to-first-response on issues
- PR merge time

## Success Principle

Velocity + reliability + responsiveness beats one-time launch hype.
